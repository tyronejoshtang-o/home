My Passion

"My hobbies include working out, as it's my form of self-expression and a source of confidence. Additionally, I enjoy reading books because I seek knowledge and am never satisfied. However, not all of my hobbies bring me enjoyment; there are activities I find more entertaining than these. I engage in these hobbies not because I want to, but because I have to. I want to become better, not compared to others, but compared to my past self. This involves sacrificing immediate desires for future benefits. As the saying goes, 'First a man makes habits, and then habits make a man."
